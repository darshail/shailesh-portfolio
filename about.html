With 25 years of experience in the education and EdTech industry, I specialize in building impactful learning products that align pedagogy, technology, and institutional goals. My work focuses on creating scalable academic ecosystems that improve learner engagement, teacher effectiveness, and educational outcomes.

Over the years, I have led multidisciplinary teams across Product Management, Learning Design, Content Development, Quality, Training, and Delivery. I strongly believe that successful educational products are built by combining strong pedagogy with user-centric product thinking and operational excellence.

Beyond product strategy and academic innovation, I enjoy mentoring teams, designing future-ready learning experiences, and exploring how AI and digital transformation can reshape education. My passion lies in creating meaningful solutions that empower both educators and learners.
